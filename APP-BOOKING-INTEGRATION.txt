At the top of App.js, add:
```javascript

```

## Step 3: Add BookingManagement to tab content

Find the tab-content div and add:
```javascript
<div className="tab-content">
  {activeTab === 'schedule' && (
    <ScheduleTable ... />
  )}
  {activeTab === 'print' && <PrintView />}

  {activeTab === 'conflicts' && <ConflictPage ... />}

```
